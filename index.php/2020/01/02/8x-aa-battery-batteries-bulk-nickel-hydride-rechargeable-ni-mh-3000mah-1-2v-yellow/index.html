8x AA battery batteries Bulk Nickel Hydride Rechargeable NI-MH 3000mAh 1.2V Yellow

Stay away from these batteries. I bought them on eBay. I only used them a few times before they would hold hardly any charge and I have now thrown many of them away.

They are much lighter in weight than what you would expect. I have a theory (but no proof) that they are partly alkaline batteries so maybe they work initially, but they don’t keep working after many recharges. However, I’m not sure if that is possible or not.
