It is in the best interests of post office workers to process as little mail as possible

It is in the best interests of post office workers to process as little mail as possible. Enough mail so that their branch doesn’t get shut down, but no more. How can this be changed so that the interests of the post office workers and the post office customers are more in alignment. Perhaps the workers can be paid partly on commission so that the more they ship the more they get paid.
