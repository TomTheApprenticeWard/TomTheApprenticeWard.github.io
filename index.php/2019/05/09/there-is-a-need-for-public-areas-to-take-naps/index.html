There is a need for public areas to take naps

There is a need for public areas to take naps, places that aren’t in the home. For example there could be a small area of the library or maybe a universities could have areas, etc. Of course there is a risk of people’s items getting stolen when they are sleeping so maybe there could be some small lockers near the sleeping area. In order to be sanitary people could bring their own small and lightweight sheet and lay it on top of the sleeping pad. They could also bring earplugs and a sleep mask if needed.
